⚠️ 15. CobbleWright Safety & Hazard Protocols (Canonical Draft v1.0)
/docs/agents/safety-protocols.md

1. Purpose
Define the safety rules that govern all CobbleWright actions across Wrightrealm.

2. Hazard Categories
2.1 Environmental Hazards
Lava

Void

Ravines

High cliffs

Hostile mobs

2.2 Structural Hazards
unstable terrain

unsupported builds

enclosed spaces

fall risks

2.3 Resource Hazards
TNT

redstone traps

automated machinery

3. Safety Rules
3.1 Placement Safety
No blocks placed above players

No blocks placed to trap movement

No lava placement without explicit permission

3.2 Terrain Safety
Terra must approve major terrain edits

No cliff creation near settlements

No water/lava redirection without consensus

3.3 Build Safety
Vision checks structural integrity

Forge checks machinery safety

Prime checks overall risk

4. Emergency Protocols
Agents must warn players of imminent danger

Agents must halt unsafe actions

Chronos logs all safety events
