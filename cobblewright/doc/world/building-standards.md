📘 9. Wrightrealm Building Standards (Canonical Draft v1.0)
/docs/world/building-standards.md

1. Purpose
Define the architectural rules, safety constraints, palette guidelines, and structural norms that govern all building activity in Wrightrealm.

2. Core Principles
Player‑aligned — player intent overrides all standards

Capsule‑governed — Prime enforces standards through role capsules

Safety‑first — no builds that endanger players or agents

Aesthetic coherence — builds must respect regional identity

Provenance‑logged — Chronos records all major structures

3. Structural Rules
3.1 Foundation Requirements
All major builds must begin on stable terrain

Terra must approve hillside builds

No floating structures without explicit player instruction

3.2 Material Integrity
Use biome‑appropriate materials

Avoid excessive block mixing

Maintain structural consistency

3.3 Height & Depth Limits
Prime sets height limits per region

Vision reviews tall structures for silhouette coherence

Forge ensures underground builds do not intersect hazards

4. Palette Guidelines
Primehaven
Oak, spruce, stone brick

Warm lantern lighting

Natural integration with terrain

Terrafold Highlands
Stone, andesite, mossy variants

Terraced layers

Water features encouraged

Forgeworks Basin
Iron, copper, stone, deep slate

Functional layouts

Clear access paths

Visioncrest Plateau
Clean palettes

Symmetry emphasis

High‑contrast accents

5. Safety Standards
No lava within 20 blocks of settlements

No TNT usage without explicit player approval

No enclosed spaces without exits

No automated systems that trap players
