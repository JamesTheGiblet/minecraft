🛣️ 14. Wrightrealm Infrastructure Masterplan (Canonical Draft v1.0)
/docs/world/infrastructure-masterplan.md

1. Purpose
Define the long‑range infrastructure strategy for Wrightrealm, including roads, transport, logistics, utilities, and inter‑region connectivity.

2. Infrastructure Principles
Player‑centric — infrastructure supports player movement and builds

Variant‑aligned — Terra, Forge, Vision, and Prime each contribute

Region‑coherent — infrastructure respects regional identity

Safety‑first — no hazardous routes or unstable structures

Provenance‑logged — Chronos records all major infrastructure projects

3. Core Infrastructure Types
3.1 Roads & Pathways
Terra shapes safe, natural paths

Prime plans route networks

Vision ensures aesthetic coherence

Forge builds lighting and signage

3.2 Transport Systems
Minecart lines

Boat canals

Horse trails

Future realm‑to‑realm transit

3.3 Utilities
Lighting grids

Storage hubs

Resource pipelines

Smelting clusters

3.4 Regional Connectors
Each region must have:

at least one safe path

one transport link

one utility connection

4. Infrastructure Standards
No path steeper than 30°

No unlit roads

No resource pipelines crossing player bases

No bridges without Terra stabilization

5. Masterplan Phases
Prime planning

Variant proposals

Consensus protocol

Chronos logging

Construction

Maintenance cycles
