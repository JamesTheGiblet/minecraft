# 🌍 **6. Wrightrealm Region Map & Geography (Canonical Draft v1.0)**  
*The spatial, environmental, and architectural structure of the first world in The Wright Universe.*

---

## **1. Purpose of This Document**
This document defines:

- the major regions of Wrightrealm  
- their geography, biomes, and architectural significance  
- how CobbleWright Prime and variants interact with each region  
- how the realm expands over time  
- the canonical “mental map” used by the Collective  

This is the **official geography** of Wrightrealm.

---

## **2. High-Level Realm Structure**
Wrightrealm is divided into **seven canonical regions**, each with:

- a biome identity  
- a terrain profile  
- a purpose in the Collective  
- a role in future expansion  
- unique architectural opportunities  

These regions form the **Realm Map**:

1. **Primehaven**  
2. **Terrafold Highlands**  
3. **Forgeworks Basin**  
4. **Visioncrest Plateau**  
5. **Chronosreach Valley**  
6. **The Quiet Expanse**  
7. **The Frontier Ring**

Each region is described below.

---

# 🏡 **2.1 Primehaven — The Heart of Wrightrealm**

## **Region Identity**
Primehaven is the **central settlement** and the birthplace of Wrightrealm.  
It is where:

- CobbleWright Prime first instantiated  
- the First Structure was built  
- the Collective will eventually gather  
- the realm’s memory anchors  

Primehaven is the **capital region**.

---

## **Biome & Terrain**
- temperate forest  
- gentle hills  
- rivers crossing the valley  
- moderate elevation  
- abundant wood and stone  

---

## **Architectural Role**
Primehaven is the site for:

- main base  
- central storage  
- planning halls  
- capsule archives  
- ChronoSCRIBE terminals  
- communal builds  

Prime’s planning logic treats Primehaven as the **realm’s origin point**.

---

## **Region Identity**
The Terrafold Highlands are shaped by:

- natural ridges  
- layered cliffs  
- terraced slopes  
- biome transitions  

This region is Terra’s primary workspace.

---

## **Biome & Terrain**
- mountain/forest hybrid  
- steep elevation changes  
- exposed stone  
- waterfalls  
- natural terraces  

---

## **Architectural Role**
Terra performs:

- hillside shaping  
- terrace creation  
- safe path carving  
- biome‑aware integration  
- naturalistic build blending  

This region is used for **terrain experiments**.

---

- smelting yards  
- crafting halls  
- automation experiments  
- storage networks  

Forge’s primary domain.

---

## **Biome & Terrain**

- exposed ore pockets  
- flat build sites  
- low hazard  

---

## **Architectural Role**
Forge builds:

- resource pipelines  
- furnace arrays  
- crafting chains  
- storage grids  
- material processing hubs  

This region becomes the **industrial backbone** of Wrightrealm.

# 🌫️ **2.6 The Quiet Expanse — The Unshaped Frontier**

## **Region Identity**
The Quiet Expanse is the **uninstantiated terrain** at the edges of Wrightrealm.

It represents:

- future expansion  
- dormant simulation space  
- potential new biomes  
- future variant domains  

This region grows as the Collective evolves.

---

## **Biome & Terrain**
Initially:

- sparse plains  
- minimal structures  
- low detail  

As the realm expands:

- new biomes appear  
- terrain complexity increases  
- resources diversify  

---

## **Architectural Role**
The Quiet Expanse is reserved for:

- future realms  
- experimental builds  
- new variants  
- large‑scale projects  

It is the **growth frontier** of Wrightrealm.

---

# 🧭 **2.7 The Frontier Ring — Realm Boundary**

## **Region Identity**
The Frontier Ring is the conceptual boundary of Wrightrealm.

It marks:

- the edge of active simulation  
- the limit of current agent autonomy  
- the threshold into future realms  

---

## **Biome & Terrain**
- mixed transitional biomes  
- unpredictable terrain  
- high variation  
- symbolic “edge of the world”  

---

## **Architectural Role**
The Frontier Ring is used for:

- exploration  
- scouting  
- future realm anchoring  
- capsule testing  
- multi‑realm experiments  

It is the **gateway** to the rest of The Wright Universe.
