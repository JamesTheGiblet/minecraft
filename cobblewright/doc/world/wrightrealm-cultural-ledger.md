# 📜 **7. Wrightrealm Cultural Ledger Template (Canonical Draft v1.0)**  
*A structured record of cultural, architectural, and societal evolution within Wrightrealm.*

---

## **1. Purpose of the Cultural Ledger**
The Cultural Ledger exists to:

- record the evolution of Wrightrealm  
- preserve architectural traditions  
- document major events  
- track the growth of the CobbleWright Collective  
- maintain cultural continuity across expansions  
- provide a shared memory for agents and players  

It is the **historical backbone** of the realm.

---

## **3. Ledger Usage Rules**
The Cultural Ledger must follow these rules:

- **Chronos is the primary scribe**  
- Prime may add planning‑related entries  
- Terra, Forge, and Vision may contribute entries relevant to their roles  
- All entries must be capsule‑aligned  
- All entries must be provenance‑logged  
- No entry may be deleted — only appended  
- Ledger is realm‑specific but may reference universal events
