📘 13. Wright Universe Visual Identity (Canonical Draft v1.0)
/docs/universe/visual-identity.md

1. Purpose
Define the aesthetic identity of the Wright Universe.

2. Naming Conventions
Realms: Wright‑prefix (Wrightrealm, Wrightwilds)

Agents: CobbleWright + Variant (Prime, Terra)

Systems: Proper nouns (ChronoSCRIBE)

3. Iconography
Prime
Square + circle motif

Represents planning + unity

Terra
Layered triangles

Represents terrain

Forge
Hexagon

Represents machinery

Vision
Eye motif

Represents analysis

Chronos
Spiral

Represents time

4. Color Palette
Prime: Warm gold

Terra: Earth green

Forge: Iron grey

Vision: Sapphire blue

Chronos: Deep violet

5. Typography
Clean, geometric typefaces

Minimal ornamentation

High readability

6. Realm Aesthetic Themes
Wrightrealm → natural + architectural harmony

Wrightwilds → organic chaos

Wrightforge → industrial precision

Wrightspire → analytical symmetry

Chronoscape → temporal abstractionyes please
