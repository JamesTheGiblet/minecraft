3. Capsule Design Principles
3.1 Clarity
Capsules must be:

explicit

unambiguous

readable

3.2 Modularity
Capsules must be:

independent

swappable

versionable

3.3 Safety
Capsules must:

prevent harm

enforce constraints

override unsafe behaviour

3.4 Provenance
Capsules must include:

version numbers

change logs

authorship

4. Capsule Versioning
Prime maintains canonical versions

Chronos logs all updates

Variants may have realm‑specific overrides

No capsule may violate universal safety rules
