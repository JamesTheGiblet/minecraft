## 🛣️ The Paths to Income

### Path 1: Direct Revenue (Simplest)

| Revenue Stream | How It Works | Estimated Monthly |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Patreon / Ko-fi** | Premium features, early access, priority support | $200 - $1,000 |
| **Server Licensing** | Sell BlockSmith to server owners (one-time or monthly) | $500 - $3,000 |
| **Custom Builds** | Charge for custom blueprints or plugins | $50 - $500/project |
| **Consulting** | Help others set up their own AI companions | $100 - $500/hour |
| **Donations** | "Buy me a coffee" — small but can add up | $20 - $200 |

**Example:** If you get 100 server owners paying $10/month = $1,000/month.

### Path 2: Community Model (Scalable)

| Revenue Stream | How It Works | Estimated Monthly |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **YouTube/Twitch** | Stream BlockSmith gameplay, tutorials, builds | $500 - $5,000+ |
| **Discord Community** | Premium Discord tiers, coaching, community challenges | $100 - $1,000+ |
| **Merch** | "BlockSmith" merch — shirts, stickers, digital assets | $50 - $500+ |

**Why this works:** You're not just selling a bot. You're building a *brand* that people want to be part of.

### Path 3: The Crypto/Token Route (Long-Term)

| Revenue Stream | How It Works | Estimated Monthly |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Token Launch** | $BLOCKSMITH token — early investors get in | Potentially $10,000+ |
| **Marketplace Fees** | Take % of every transaction on your platform | Potentially $1,000+ |
| **Staking** | Community stakes tokens to unlock features | Potentially $500+ |

**Why this works:** This is the "big vision" path. It requires more work upfront but has the highest ceiling.
